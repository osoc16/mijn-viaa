VIAA

The Flemish Institute for Archiving (VIAA) seeks to archive the digital heritage of Flanders in a sustainable manner and to make it accessible to everyone.

VIAA digitises, stores and provides access to audiovisual material, photos, documents, etc., together with partners from the cultural, heritage and media sectors.
